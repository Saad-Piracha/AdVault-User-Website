1. ABOUT ADVAULT
----------------
AdVault is Pakistan’s leading digital out-of-home (DOOH) technology platform. We are digitizing outdoor advertising by placing smart, high-resolution screens inside premium ride-hailing vehicles.

AdVault provides brands with a direct, high-attention channel to reach seated, engaged passengers during their daily commutes.

2. WHY IN-CAB DIGITAL ADVERTISING?
----------------------------------
* Captive Audience: Passengers spend an average of 20 to 45 minutes seated in a quiet, distraction-free environment.
* Smart Screens: High-definition, bright, interactive screens designed for optimal viewing distance.
* Geofencing Capability: Show target advertisements only when the vehicle drives through specified locations or neighborhoods.
* Real-Time Analytics: Access campaign performance, total plays, heatmaps, and reach analytics directly from the cloud.

3. TECH STACK & CAPABILITIES
-----------------------------
* Platform Core: React, TypeScript, Supabase, Postgres, Cloudflare R2, and Android hardware clients.
* Campaign Scheduling: Control date ranges, active hours, frequency, and display priority.
* Seamless Fallback: Automatically shows high-value default ads when a vehicle travels outside your designated campaign zone.
* Remote Sync: Campaign creatives can be updated, paused, or replaced across the entire fleet within minutes.

4. CAMPAIGN PLANS & TIERS
--------------------------
* Pilot Plan: Perfect for early campaigns and localized testing. Ideal for test-and-learn campaigns across selected vehicles/zones. (Custom Quoted)
* Growth Plan: For scaling brands requiring multiple geofences, advanced schedules, and detailed reporting. (Custom Quoted)
* Enterprise Plan: Large-scale rollouts with dedicated strategic support, API integrations, and fleet-wide deployment. (Custom Quoted)

5. CREATIVE ASSET SPECIFICATIONS
--------------------------------
* Video Formats: H.264 MP4 (high profile), 1080p or 720p, 30fps. Duration of 10s, 15s, or 30s recommended.
* Image Formats: High-quality PNG or JPG files matching screen resolution.
* QR Codes: Highly encouraged to link passengers to promotions, apps, or landing pages.
